I'm Ben--in case you missed that. Welcome to my portfolio. I'm a writer & content strategist.

User experience is the central focus that makes my work cohere. I'm happy writing useful, human, testable copy for interactive interfaces.

The internet should be easy, searchable, and usable; it doesn't matter if you need a freelancer (ahem), you're shopping, or learning more about the big world. The words we choose can make it or break it.

As a UX writer and content strategist, I can help make interfaces consistent, clear, and easy to understand. Because designers and developers shoudln't have to worry about that.

UX writing is a new, specialized field, so people don't always know what to call this type of writing. I write copy, run usability studies, A/B test, iterate, and perfect. I've been called a technical writer, content writer, and a "genius." But that last one was from my mom. I currently have a full-time position, but I'm available for interesting projects on the side.
